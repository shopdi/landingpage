FEATURES

For buyers

Shopdi is the first eCommerce platform where customers can find premium products at reasonable prices. Shopdi's products work under the Gamification mechanism with unlimited discounts. With each click to see the price, the price will be reduced by a corresponding amount. Therefore, buyers can absolutely buy the product at the best price in the market and decide the time, price level and favourite store to buy while still keeping the same benefits as when buying at offline shops..

For sellers

Shopdi supports sellers with all features of a traditional e-commerce platform such as: the easy management of SKUs, extensibility of APIs for real-time inventory updates and the complete automaticity of the buying and selling process. Shopdi technicians are always available to assist the store with the product listing and to connect APIs to the merchant's logistics management system. In addition, Shopdi also helps sellers to sell more products with a secret pricing mechanism without affecting sales profits.

For investors

Shopdi promotes the development of tokenomics by allowing investors to use their tokens to make product purchases with the value of their tokens. In addition, the growth of the community is ensured by allowing accumulation or exchange of monetary value into tokens to make transactions right on Shopdi.

For the CSR community

Shopdi develops a space where philanthropy is transparent. As a connection between givers and receivers with blockchain application, Shopdi ensures that donors can easily access information related to their own transactions, resolving doubts relating to whether the transaction was actually used for charity or not. Shopdi's technology will show entirely and accurately details of the flow of transactions to ensure the transparency and authenticity of the donation.
