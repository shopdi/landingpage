ABOUT US

Shopdi is the first e-commerce platform that uses blockchain technology and NFT to make transaction and product information transparent, putting user experience first, towards building a safe intermediary community for both buyers and sellers to perform transactions and exchange goods

Vision

Shopdi is becoming the trusted world's leading e-commerce platform.

BUSINESS MODEL

Ecommerce

Shopdi is an ecommerce platform specializing in high-end products as well as limited edition items, putting user experience first. Shopdi is not only issuing NFTs for limited items but also organizing campaigns to call for pre-orders for trendy products. Besides, through Shopdi's secondary market, users can exchange like-new items.

Blockchain

Shopdi also allows users to buy, sell, pay and transact with a voucher, Shopdi Coin, making cross-border purchases becoming Fast - Convenient - Secure. Moreover, transparency is top respect, especially when applying Blockchain technology to public product details about products as well as charity campaigns.

Shoppertainment

Shopdi provides enjoyable and funny shopping experiences for users by integrating gamification activities into the purchase process on the platform. A membership ranking system increases benefits for both sellers and buyers.

FEATURES

For buyers

Shopdi provides enjoyable shopping experiences by integrating gamification activies that also brings opportunities for buyers to get premium products at surprising price. Besides, the polices toward to buyers providing benefits such as: Shopdi Coin rewarding, Discount, Voucher, Accumulate points, pre-order...

For sellers

Shopdi provides a functional toolset that helps sellers creating advertisiment campaigns easily. The in-depth analysis database system will deliver the ads to the right audience, thereby increasing the conversion rate for the seller. Moreover, with the special membership classification policy from Shopdi, sellers will be supported in Advertising, Events, Communication, Sales Support...

For investor

Shopdi allows transactions and payments with Shopdi Coin making cross-border purchases easier. We support users staking to gain credit, and members can support each other to buy products in installments. In addition, non-vendor users are allowed to resell or exchange certain products on Shopdi's secondary exchange market.

For CSR

Shopdi develops a space where donors can create charity campaigns. Through the Blockchain network, entire details about campaigns can easily be accessed.
